Space Palette Pro

A Visual Music Instrument

Instructions

Finger paint on the pressure-sensitive pads.

Use the headphones to listen.

Select presets with this touchscreen.

As a Visual Instrument

Your fingers are three-dimensional paintbrushes.

Each pad draws a different style of visuals.

Finger pressure changes the size of the visuals.

As a Musical Instrument

Your fingers strum the strings of a virtual harp.

Each pad plays a different type of sound.

Lower pitches to the left, higher pitches to the right.

Faster notes at the top, slower notes at the bottom.

It's Both! A Visual Music Instrument

Each finger plays music and visuals at the same time.

Each preset changes the behavior of all four pads.

Hints

Vary finger pressure from a light caress to very hard.

You can press really hard, it won't damage the pad.

Drag your fingers around like you're finger painting.

Explore the entire pad, vertically and horizontally.

Try different presets.
