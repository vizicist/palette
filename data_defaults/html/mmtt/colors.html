Registration Colors for MultiMultiTouchTouch

These colors are used to identify the areas of a Space Palette configuration. You create an image using these colors, and give it to Space Palette to tell it about the areas you want to use. Each area becomes a separate multitouch surface. The session IDs in the TUIO messages indicate the area - e.g. TUIO session IDs from area 5 will start at 5000, from area 6 will start at 6000, etc.
